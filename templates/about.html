Production, efficient local food supply chain & Sustainablity.

Local food outlets often face challenges in managing a consistent and reliable supply of fresh produce. Farmers may not know where to sell their goods or what items are in demand, leading to food waste and missed opportunities. Additionally, food outlets struggle with tracking the quality and quantity of supplies needed for their customers.

The website will serve as a platform where local farmers can register their details, including their harvest quantity and quality, and local food outlets can view available produce and place orders
